Composing Components (tutorial 2-3)

Let's create scaffold two more components using the om/component convenience macro:

```clojure (defn comment-list [app] (om/component (dom/div #js {:className "commentList"} "Hello, world! I am a CommentList.")))

(defn comment-form [app] (om/component (dom/div #js {:className "commentForm"} "Hello, world! I am a CommentForm."))) ```

and add them to our comment-box component.

clojure (defn comment-box [app] (om/component (dom/div #js {:className "commentBox"} (dom/h1 nil "Comments") (om/build comment-list app) (om/build comment-form app))))
